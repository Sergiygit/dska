why steel, not wood

Fast installation with custom design

Quick and easy steel erection with proper length, punching pattern, and labeling. Engineers and architects assist in creating unique designs or converting from wood to steel in digital projects

Corrosion, mold &
vermin resistance

Galvanized steel is protected from corrosion and damage by a zinc coating. It is not a food source for vermin such as termites, making it durable. Steel does not absorb moisture, preventing mold growth

Strong &
Dimensional stable

Steel framing is dimensionally stable and does not expand/contract with moisture, or shrink, split, warp, crack or creep. Steel is stronger and more durable, reducing maintenance and repair costs over time

Safe from fire,
hurricanes and floods

Steel is resistant to severe weather and safer from fire as it does not burn, emit toxic gases and does not contribute to the spread of fire. It is more resistant to natural disasters such as hurricanes and floods
